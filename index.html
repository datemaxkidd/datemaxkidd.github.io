About Me

I grew up in Northwest Indiana where I attended Boone Grove from kindergarten until I graduated high school in 2018. While at Boone Grove I was the captain and goalkeeper for the varsity soccer program, and completed a Computer Technology Vocational program through the Porter County Career Center. I earned two Information Technology jobs through the vocational program, one for a non-profit early childhood education center, Hilltop Neighborhood House and one for my high school after I graduated. Upon graduation from Boone Grove High School I moved to Muncie, Indiana where I continued my education at Ball State University and majored in computer science. During my senior year capstone project, I was a part of a four person team that designed and developed an application for RefReps Education .
